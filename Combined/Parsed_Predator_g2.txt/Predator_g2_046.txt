ext. knoll - day

schaefer races down the hillside, joined by dillon, leaving hawkins to 
cover them. bullets burst around their feet as they run low, firing 
short bursts to his left and right.

he fires the undermounted grenade launcher, sending a round into a gun 
position in the trees. still running he breaks open the breach, 
slamming in another 40mm round from the cartridge pack at his waist.

blain and mac fire the heavy machine guns, laying down a withering 
curtain of lead, shredding the camp, taking out five guerrillas at 
once.
